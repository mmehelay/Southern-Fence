About Southern Fence

Southern Fence has been delivering unparalleled customer service and quality since 2005. Our number one focus is to provide top quality fence installation services along with the best custom service the industry has ever seen. We are experienced fence installation experts with over 15 years of experience.

Our number one goal is and will continue to be our quality workmanship. We guarantee that every fence installation will be completed with the highest quality standards. We will take time to listen to your needs and concerns. We will make sure to exceed your expectations in every aspect of the fence installation on your property.

At Southern Fence, we measure success not by the fences we build, but by the smiles we create and the trust we earn with every satisfied client

Blake Schwartz CEO-Founder
