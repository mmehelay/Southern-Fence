Why Choose Vinyl as Your Fence of Choice?

Vinyl Fences are Uniquely Durable

Vinyl fences offer property owners a long-term solution that can last anywhere from 20 to 30 years or more with proper care. This impressive longevity is a result of several compositional factors and technological advancements that have been integrated into the manufacturing of vinyl fencing products.

High-quality polyvinyl chloride (PVC) composition is a big part of this durability. PVC is a type of plastic that is known for its strength, flexibility, and resistance to environmental factors. One of the key components that contribute to the durability of vinyl fencing is titanium dioxide (TiO2), a pigment that provides UV protection. This additive is crucial for preventing the vinyl from breaking down under the sun's harsh rays, which can lead to fading, cracking, and brittleness over time. Additionally, some vinyl fences are manufactured using a process called co-extrusion, which creates a two-layer product consisting of a UV-resistant outer layer and a strong, impact-resistant inner layer.

Choose From Many Designs and Styles

Homeowners have so many vinyl fencing styles to choose from. Traditional white picket fences are among the most popular choices, and you can even choose from a variety of colors, from neutral shades like tan and gray to more striking hues that mimic the appearance of stained wood.

Wood grain textures are another innovative finish available in vinyl fencing, providing the natural beauty of wood without the maintenance. These fences are designed to replicate the look and feel of real wood, including patterns and color variations, making them an attractive alternative for those who appreciate the wood aesthetic but want to avoid the upkeep associated with it.

In addition to wood grain textures, vinyl fencing can also come in smooth or embossed finishes. From privacy fences with solid panels to decorative picket styles and shadow box designs, the versatility of vinyl fencing enables homeowners to create unique solutions that improve both security and curb appeal.
