Wood Fencing Installation

We offer various types, colors, and styles to suit your preferences, including picket, ranch, and split rail designs. The beauty of wood fencing is in its versatility. If you prefer a traditional look, a white picket fence might be perfect. If you need something to frame a larger property, a ranch-style fence can provide that open and rustic feel. Split rail fences add charm to both large and small properties, while taller wooden fences offer more privacy. With the right stain or paint, a wood fence can be customized to match your home’s exterior. Plus, wood has a warmth that other materials can’t quite match, making it a favorite for many residents in the South Carolina lowcountry.

Chain Link Fences

Chain link fencing is known for its durability and low maintenance, and that’s what makes them an ideal solution for both residential and commercial spaces. They’re perfect for pet enclosures, defining property lines, and securing larger areas. Available in different heights and coated in colors like green or black, chain link fences can be customized to blend into the background or stand out, depending on your preference. We also offer privacy slats, which can be added to chain link fences to give you more seclusion while retaining the fence’s functionality.

Wrought Iron, Aluminum, and Other Metal Fencing

Wrought iron and aluminum fences offer elegance and strength that other materials can’t promise. These fences are ideal for homeowners looking to add a touch of classic charm to their property. Wrought iron fences, with their intricate designs and sturdy frames, can give any property a distinguished look. Aluminum fencing provides a similar aesthetic at a more affordable price point and requires less maintenance, making it a great alternative. These metal fences are excellent for security, adding curb appeal, and offering a long-lasting boundary around your property. They’re also highly customizable, allowing you to choose from various styles and finishes.
