Choose Your Chain Link Fencing Style

Chain link fences are popular because of their durability and range of material options, colors, coatings, and link styles. Traditionally, these fences are made from galvanized steel, a durable material that withstands harsh weather conditions and provides a strong barrier. For those seeking a more corrosion-resistant option, vinyl-coated chain link fences are available, which also come in a variety of colors such as black, green, brown, and white.

These coatings not only add to the fence's durability by protecting against rust and corrosion, but also give them a look that can suit your unique property. The actual link styles can also vary, with standard diamond patterns being the most common, but there are options for smaller or tighter weaves, which offer increased privacy and security.

Chain Link Fences Can Resist Hilton Head’s Weather Patterns

The galvanized steel or aluminum used in chain link fences provides a strong, rust-resistant barrier that can withstand the high humidity and salt air of coastal South Carolina. When coated with vinyl, these fences gain an additional layer of protection against corrosion, fading, and weathering, further extending their lifespan.
