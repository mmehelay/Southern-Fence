Choose Your Fence - We Do The Rest

Life is too short to install your own fencing system. We’ve worked on thousands of properties, and we are the Lowcountry’s go-to experts for fence installation. We will discuss your needs with you, order the materials, and carefully install an enclosure that will last for many years.

Each fencing style has unique challenges that require expert skill. Wood fences need to be measured, anchored in such a way that the wood won’t rot or move, and sealed and stained or painted. Chain link fences must be purchased in the correct amounts, linked together with durable and reliable hardware, and finished according to the owner’s preference. Vinyl, while remaining one of the most popular choices on the market, requires specialized tools to cut and install – it also requires specialized experience to build the fence and anchor it. And aluminum needs a high end powder coating added via electrostatic application.

All you need to do is let us know if you want a wood fence, metal fence, living fence, or other style, and we’ll handle every step of the process with minimal damage to your lawn.
