Ranch and Split Rail Fences

For larger properties or those with a more rural feel, ranch-style and split rail fences offer a touch of rustic charm. These fences are popular on farms and acreages but look just as lovely on a residential property. Ranch fences typically have horizontal rails and are great for defining property lines without blocking the view. Split rail fences provide a natural look with their rough-hewn wood rails and are a perfect choice for those who want a fence with a laid-back, country aesthetic. Both styles allow for an open feel while giving your property a defined boundary.

Aluminum fencing is a popular choice for its combination of style, resilience, and affordability. This type of fencing has the classic look of wrought iron but without the upkeep.
