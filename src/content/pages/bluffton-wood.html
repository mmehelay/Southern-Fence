Wood Types For All Uses and Budgets

Many of our customers go for pressure-treated pine or spruce, since it’s affordable, durable, and receives paint or stain nicely. We can also install a cedar fence on your property - these fences are naturally gorgeous, and they are more resistant to the elements, bugs and decay. You won’t need to treat the wood as often, and it does look higher-end.

More exotic woods like redwood are at the higher end of the spectrum, and while not very common in South Carolina, a redwood fence is an amazing opportunity to stand out in your neighborhood.

All things considered, we will want to have a conversation about your goals, style preferences, and budget – we can help you choose the right kind of wood.
