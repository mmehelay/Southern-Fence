Wood Fence Installation

Despite the humid weather, wood fences will always be popular in South Carolina. They look beautiful, come in many designs, and always complement a Lowcountry property. Pine can be treated to resist pests and weather, cedar naturally repels bugs, and redwood fencing immediately increases your home’s property value. From a classic picket fence to a tall privacy fence, we can make it with wood.

Metal Fencing

For something a little more long term, metal fencing is a good option. Aluminum fencing is lightweight, low-maintenance, and can give off the look of wrought iron without the hassle. It’s a great option for both decorative front yards and pool areas. If you want the real deal, wrought iron offers timeless beauty and added security, though it does require occasional maintenance to prevent rust. We are happy to walk you through treatment and maintenance options.

For those who just need something simple and effective, chain link fencing is always on the table. We can install chain link fences of varying heights in Yemassee, and we can also add gates and gate operators.

Vinyl Fencing

If you want as little maintenance as possible, vinyl fencing is the way to go. Resistant to weather and wear, vinyl comes in a variety of colors and styles to match your home. Whether you need privacy or just want to define your property lines, vinyl fences last for years without needing to be repainted or repaired.
