Choose The Right Fencing Material

Choosing the right fencing material for your property in Sheldon, SC can feel like a big decision, but we’re here to help guide you. Each material comes with its own benefits and can be tailored to suit your specific needs.

Wood Fence Installation in Sheldon

Wood fencing offers a timeless look that many homeowners love. You can choose from a variety of woods like cedar, pine, and redwood, and each has different levels of durability. Cedar resists insects and rot naturally, and looks beautiful while doing so. Pine is often more affordable and looks great, but it may need more upkeep. Redwood isn’t as common in South Carolina, but it gives off a premium look and feel. We treat, prime, and paint wood fences for our customers (unless they prefer the unpainted look).

Metal Fence Options: Aluminum, Iron and Chain Link

Aluminum fences are lightweight and low-maintenance, and they can provide the look of wrought iron without the rust or maintenance. They’re perfect for pool areas or decorative front yards. Wrought iron fences do require some maintenance to prevent rust, but for many property owners, it’s worth it. We also install many chain link fences throughout Beaufort County.

Vinyl Fencing Installation

Vinyl fencing is resistant to weather and doesn’t need much upkeep. They come in various colors and styles, so we can match your home’s aesthetic. It's often more expensive upfront, but it pays off in the long run by not needing painting or repairs.

What Style Complements Your Property?

If you have a more open and rural property in Sheldon, ranch or split rail fencing might be a perfect match. We can create a rustic look while still providing a nice boundary. Picket fences are great if you want a classic and welcoming feel in your front yard. You can even go with a tasteful scalloped design to add some visual interest.

For a more decorative look, ornamental fencing—whether in wrought iron or aluminum—adds a dramatic element to any property. If you need a little more privacy but still want style, shadow box fencing is a great option. This design alternates panels on each side, offering both privacy and a beautiful view from either side.

We can show you examples, discuss pricing, and help you figure out the materials that best fit your needs.
