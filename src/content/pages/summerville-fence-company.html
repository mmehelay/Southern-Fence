A Variety of Fence Installation Services in Summerville, SC

Metal and Aluminum

From chain link fences to aluminum fencing that imitates traditional wrought iron fences, we can install a beautiful or practical metal fence for you. These fences are durable, corrosion resistant, and come in a variety of colors and designs. We can also apply a powder coating to your metal fence – these help resist harsh coastal weather, provide multiple color options, and protect against chips and more.

Wood Fencing

From cedar to pine, redwood, and spruce, wooden fences are still popular among homeowners in the greater Charleston area. They will always be timeless and beautiful, even if they are more susceptible to the elements and pests than more modern solutions. We can install a wooden fence for you (picket, split rail and more), finish it with either paint or a stain, and help you with ongoing maintenance.

Vinyl Fencing

Vinyl is highly resistant to weathering, decay, and insect damage, and it maintains its appearance without the need for painting or staining. We have installed countless cost-effective vinyl fences for our clients, and we would love to help you find the perfect style and color within your budget.

Pool fences, high privacy fences, and even living fences are within our realm of expertise. We would love to work with you to find the right style of fence for your home, and since we have great relationships with our suppliers, we can have materials in very quickly.
