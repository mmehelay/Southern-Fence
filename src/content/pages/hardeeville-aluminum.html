Aluminum is an Excellent Choice For a Number of Reasons

Enjoy 30+ Years of Longevity

Aluminum may not be quite as strong as steel, but unless you’re in a commercial area where cars will be bumping into your fence, aluminum is more than strong enough. It can withstand the sometimes severe weather of South Carolina’s coastal region, and it won’t rust, rot, or fall prey to insects. Because of its long lifespan, aluminum is genuinely one of the best values in the fencing market. With a professional installation from our team of experts, you can expect your aluminum fence to last for many decades.

Aluminum Fencing Doesn’t Require Regular Maintenance

Colors and finishes are applied to aluminum fences via a high tech powder coating, and this chip and scratch-resistant coating won’t need to be re-applied unless you want to change the fence’s color or replace damaged sections after a tree falls down, or something of that nature. You don’t have to paint, stain, or seal it, and you won’t have to replace individual pickets like you would with a pine fence.
