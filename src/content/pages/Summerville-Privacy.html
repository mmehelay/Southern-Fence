Benefits of Privacy Fencing

If allowed by your HOA or township, a privacy fence can dramatically improve your quality of life. You can shield your property and family from prying eyes, cut down on noise pollution, and better enjoy your patio, deck, lawn and pool. You can also reduce the risk of crime or trespassing with a privacy fence, and you won’t have neighboring dogs straying into your yard. If you call us to install a beautiful and high quality privacy fence on your property, both your curb appeal and home valuation will improve.

Styles and Material Options

We have experience with many different fencing materials, and we are happy to speak with you about the pros and cons of each. Vinyl is one of the most popular privacy fencing options on the market – it doesn’t require maintenance other than a periodic wash, and it comes with various color options, some of which mimic real wood. It also lasts for decades and can withstand the hot, moist climate of Summerville. Furthermore, it’s cost-effective.

Wood is a timeless option that will also be in high demand. We can seal or stain the wood to resist UV rays, moisture and bugs, and we can speak with you about the cost and benefits of different woods like pressure-treated pine, cedar, oak, and more. Aluminum is not a common material for privacy fences, but we have done semi-private fencing structures with aluminum materials. If you have any specific requests, let us know - we want you to love your fence.

Get a Durable Privacy Fence That Lasts For Decades

We only install high quality fences that will last for many years. As far as the materials themselves are concerned, vinyl privacy fences typically last the longest – you will often save money in the end when you choose vinyl. Redwood and cedar fences typically last longer than pine, but they are a bit more expensive. That said, we can increase the lifespan by staining the wood or applying a fresh seal every few years. We will speak with you about what to expect when we provide a quote.
