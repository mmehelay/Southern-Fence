Vinyl is the most popular choice for pool fences, since they last for decades and come in a variety of colors. You don’t have to paint or seal vinyl, and we can use panels for privacy, pickets for a classic look, or horizontal rails.

Design Options For Your Pool Fence

The design of your pool fence should balance looks and function. The height of the fence is an important consideration for safety; most local laws require pool fences to be at least 4 feet tall. Some homeowners choose higher fences for added privacy and security as long as HOAs and local laws allow.

The spacing between the fence slats or pickets is another crucial design aspect. To prevent small children and pets from squeezing through, the gaps should not exceed 4 inches. For those seeking more privacy, solid panel vinyl or wooden fences might be the preferred option, while aluminum or wrought iron fences are ideal for those who want a balance of safety and unobstructed views of their pool area.
