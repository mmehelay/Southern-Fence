Privacy Fence Materials to Choose From in Hilton Head

Wood is always a popular choice for privacy fences. It’s naturally beautiful, versatile, and durable – depending on the type of wood. Cedar and Redwood, for instance, resist rot and can last for many years. Pressure treated lumber is the same way; pine, on the other hand, may need more maintenance. You’ll have to paint or stain your wood, of course, but it’s always a beautiful option.

Vinyl fencing is a great option due to its low maintenance and long lifespan. It resists fading, cracking, and insect damage. While the initial cost might be higher than wood, its durability and minimal upkeep make it a cost-effective choice over time. Given the humid climate and sunshine in Hilton Head, we recommend that you at least consider a vinyl privacy fence.

Aluminum and steel provide strong, durable fencing options that mimic the appearance of traditional wrought iron without the maintenance. These materials do resist rust and corrosion, but they do not offer the same level of privacy as wood or vinyl unless designed with privacy panels.
