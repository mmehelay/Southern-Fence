Why Install an Aluminum Fence in Okatie, SC?

Aluminum Fencing Lasts For Decades

Aluminum fences are designed to last for several decades, with many installations standing strong for 30 years or more. This comes from aluminum's inherent resistance to rust and corrosion – a particularly helpful trait in the humid and salt-air prone environment of Okatie, SC. Unlike iron or steel, aluminum does not oxidize when exposed to moisture, and the protective powder coating applied to aluminum fencing shields it from the elements even further. In short, you can reasonably expect at least thirty years, if not much more, out of your aluminum fence.

Aluminum Fencing Comes in Many Styles

If you want the elegant look of wrought iron but seek a more affordable and maintenance-free alternative, aluminum fencing provides an excellent imitation with similar visual appeal. Aluminum fences can also be made to mimic the appearance of wood – enjoy pine, redwood, or other wood replicas without the fear of insects or rot. You can also get traditional picket designs, a high end scalloped fence, spear tops, flat topics, ornamental fences, and many more designs made out of material. The options really are incredible.

Aluminum Fencing Requires No Maintenance

All you need to do is hose the fence down with water and soap from time to time (although in a rainy climate, even that may not be necessary). You won’t have to repaint your fence, you don't need to seal it, and you really don’t need to re-apply the powder coating on your aluminum fence. You won’t even need to replace individual panels or pickets, as the aluminum frame can withstand the elements better than nearly any other fencing material.

The Value of an Aluminum Fence is Hard To Beat

Aluminum is cheaper than wrought iron, but more expensive than entry-level raw materials like pine – this is the perfect place to be. You won’t have to pay for painting or repairs in the future, and you won’t need to worry about replacing the fence for at least several decades. Aluminum is the right option for homeowners who understand the value of investing in premium materials that will save money and hassle in the long run.
