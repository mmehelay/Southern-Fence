Why Install an Aluminum Fence in Beaufort, SC?

Aluminum Has Incredible Longevity

Aluminum fencing is known to last for an incredible long time, and homeowners won’t need to worry about maintenance or replacement any time soon. Aluminum's qualities allow it to last for decades without the wear and tear that affects many other fencing materials. Aluminum does not rust, rot, or deteriorate when exposed to moisture, which is a common downfall of materials like wood and iron. Aluminum's natural protective oxide coating, which shields it from environmental damage, makes it one of the most durable solutions on the market.

Aluminum fences can be installed with tall privacy panels and reduced spacing between pickets to block visibility and access. Decorative designs can include intricate patterns, scrolls, and finials, and other touches.

Aluminum Fences Come in Many Colors and Design Options

There’s an aluminum color or design perfect for all preferences and architectural styles. Unlike traditional fencing materials that may offer limited color choices, aluminum fences can be powder-coated in a variety of finishes, from classic black to green, bronze, white, and more. This powder coating not only adds color, but also increases the fence's resistance to fading and chipping. Your color or finish will look vibrant and new for many years.

As far as design is concerned, aluminum fencing gives you a lot to choose from. You can choose a classic picket fence design, contemporary to compliment a modern home, and privacy even privacy panels. You can choose a wrought iron-imitation fence, decorative aluminum with curved pickets, scrolls and post caps, and pretty much anything else that piques your interest.

Aluminum Is Uniquely Suited For Beaufort, SC's Climate

High temperatures, significant sun exposure, humidity, and the potential for severe storms demand a material that can withstand these conditions without degrading. Aluminum fencing is ideally suited for Beaufort's climate due to its resistance to corrosion, rust, and deterioration from UV exposure. Unlike materials that may warp, rot, or rust in these environments, aluminum maintains its condition.

Enjoy Low Maintenance

You can forget regular maintenance. Unlike wood, which may need regular staining or painting, or iron that might require rust prevention treatments, aluminum fences need little to no upkeep to retain their appearance and durability. The occasional rinse with a hose to remove dirt and debris is typically all that's needed to keep an aluminum fence looking great.

Aluminum's Pricing Compared To Other Materials

Aluminum fencing is competitively priced, and it would be a more cost-effective option when compared to other materials like wrought iron or high-end wood fences. While the initial investment in aluminum fencing may be higher than some types of wood fencing or even vinyl, its durability, low maintenance requirements, and longevity can make it more economical over the long term. Especially if you have specific design requests, aluminum is most likely your best option.
