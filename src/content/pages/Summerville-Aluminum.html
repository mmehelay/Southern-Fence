Aluminum Fences Are Remarkably Durable

While aluminum can cost a bit more than wood up front, the return on your investment will be worth it. Aluminum can last for many decades. Unlike iron, aluminum does not rust, making it ideal for the humid climate of Summerville - the fence will remain strong and beautiful over time. Furthermore, aluminum fences withstand UV rays, rain and other environmental factors, so your risk of warping and fading is quite low. You won’t have to worry about replacing rotten boards, repainting or restaining your fence.

Durable Finishing Options

Aluminum fences are finished with a powder coating that protects the fence from abrasions, moisture, UV rays, rust and more. We can use any number of colors and textures, and if the fence needs a touch-up in a decade or two, it’s easy to have a new powder coat added. The powder coating is electromagnetically applied, and the dry powder is placed under heat to form a protective skin on the aluminum.
