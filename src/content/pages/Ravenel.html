Wood Fence Installation in Ravenel

Wood fences offer a practical option and a timeless look. In most cases, wood fencing is affordable and durable, making it one of the leading options in the fence market. One significant advantage of wood fences is their natural aesthetic, which can complement various architectural styles and blend nicely with outdoor landscapes. In short, wood fences look great, especially alongside a traditional home.

When properly treated and maintained, a well-constructed wood fence can withstand the elements for many years, and on the South Carolina coast, we understand that the weather can be rough and unpredictable. This longevity is further enhanced by the availability of different types of wood that are suitable for fencing. Common options include cedar, known for its natural resistance to decay and insects, making it ideal for humid climates. Redwood is another popular choice due to its durability and attractive reddish hue. Pine is more affordable and can be treated to improve its resistance to weathering.

From white picket fences to ranch-style fencing or other styles, we are happy to help.

Vinyl Fences in Ravenel

Unlike wood fences, vinyl fences do not require staining or painting. This makes them very easy for any property. Vinyl fences are also very durable, and they carry a simple, adaptable style that works with just about any house. It is resistant to rotting, warping, and insect damage, ensuring longevity even in harsh weather conditions. Vinyl fences are also available in various styles and colors, offering flexibility to match different architectural designs and personal preferences. On top of that, you can clean it easily with soap and water.

Aluminum Fencing

Aluminum fencing offers numerous benefits, including durability and minimal maintenance. It is highly resistant to rust and corrosion, making it ideal for all climates, including coastal areas. Aluminum fences are lightweight yet sturdy, providing security without the bulkiness of traditional materials.Additionally, aluminum fences come in a variety of styles and colors, offering versatility in design to suit different architectural preferences. Aluminum fences are a practical choice for both residential and commercial properties in need of a practical, attractive fence.

We Handle All The Construction Requirements

Installing a fence involves several construction requirements to ensure durability and compliance with local regulations. First, proper planning and measurement are crucial to determine property lines and ensure the fence is correctly positioned. Digging post holes to the appropriate depth, typically about one-third of the post height, is essential for stability. Installing sturdy posts with concrete footing to prevent leaning or shifting is necessary, especially in areas with varying soil conditions.
